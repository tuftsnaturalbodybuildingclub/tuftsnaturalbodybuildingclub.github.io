What we do

Group lifts

No fixed schedule, but we organize sessions to train together in groups for good company. Whether you lift at the Tufts gym or in Davis Square, there's someone to lift with.

Posing & stage prep

Chasing a natural show? We run posing practice and prep guidance to get you stage-ready. Also shoutouts to get support for your upcoming show(s)!

Safe dieting

Crash diets and reckless cuts can wreck your hormones and your health. Members who've been through it help your approach in a way that's sustainable, physically and mentally.

Include everyone

"Natural" means we don't abuse performance-enhancing drugs. Medical exemptions and gender-affirming care aren't abuse. All people from all backgrounds are welcome! We can also point you to inclusive federations like the IATBP.

Watch parties & talk

We gather to stream and talk about the big shows, from Mr. Olympia and the Arnold Classic to national natural competitions. Come argue and talk all things bodybuilding, no experience required.

Help eachother out!

Whether you need a tripod, advice, or help breaking through a plateau. This is a community for like minded people to help eachother! This stuff is hard!
