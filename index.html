About

Strength, earned honestly.

We're a student-run club at Tufts for anyone interested in natural — meaning drug-free — strength training and bodybuilding. Natural gains are slow and earned, and that's the whole point: we're here for the long game.

All experience levels are welcome. Never touched a barbell? Great. Been lifting for years? Also great. We train together, share what works, and keep each other accountable. Compete on stage one day if you want to — or just get stronger with good people.

What we do

A few ways to get involved

Group lifts

No fixed schedule, but we organize sessions to train together in groups for good company. Whether you lift at the Tufts gym or in Davis Square, there's someone to lift with.

Dieting done safe

Crash diets and reckless cuts can wreck your hormones and your health. Members who've been through it help you cut or bulk in a way that's sustainable, physically and mentally.

Posing & stage prep

Chasing a natural show? We run posing practice and prep guidance to get you stage-ready. Also shoutouts to get support for your upcoming show(s)!

Nutrition & recovery

Workshops on eating for your goals, sleep, and training smart so progress actually sticks.

Open to everyone

"Natural" means we don't abuse performance-enhancing drugs, nothing more. Medical exemptions and gender-affirming care aren't abuse, and they are more than welcome here. Want to compete? We'll point you to inclusive organizations like the IATBP.

Watch parties & talk

We gather to stream and talk about the big shows, from Mr. Olympia and the Arnold Classic to national natural competitions. Come argue and talk all things bodybuilding, no experience required.
